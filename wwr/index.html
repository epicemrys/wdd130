North Fork

The North Fork rafting is a Spring trip fed by snowmelt and is free-flowing. This is the only Fork of the American River that is not dam-controlled. The rafting season on the North Fork typically starts April 1st and may run until mid-June.

Snake River

The Snake River is known for having the deepest gorge in the United States, Hell's Canyon. This gorge reaches up to 7,900 ft deep at certain points. Boulders and rock slides have rolled into the river to create the biggest white water rafting rapids in the Pacific Northwest.
